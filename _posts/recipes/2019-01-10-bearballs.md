- ½ cup peanut butter
- ½ cup honey
- 1 cup powdered milk
- 1 cup old fashioned rolled oats

mix together and form into balls, roll  in

- 1⁄4 cup toasted wheat germ

Optionally add cocoa powder or chocolate chips.
