Adapted from https://theviewfromgreatisland.com/chive-and-parsley-pesto-recipe/.
We thought this was delicious, but also consider the following recipe a starting
point.

* 2 c packed chives
* 1 c packed parsley
* 1 clove garlic
* 1 handful toasted walnuts (10 minutes at 350)
* 1 oz parmesan
* juice of 1 lemon (2-3 Tbsp)
* 1/2 t salt
* 1/2 c oil

Add all ingredients except oil to the food processor.  Mix well.  Slowly
drizzle oil while machine is on.
