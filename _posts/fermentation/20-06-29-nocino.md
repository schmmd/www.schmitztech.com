## 2020 Nocino

![Nocino, after two days]({{ baseurl }}/assets/img/nocino-day2.jpg)

### Seattle Times Nocino

* 2 lb walnuts (about 50-60 walnuts)
* 2 cinnamon sticks
* 1/16 t ground cloves
* 1 vanilla bean (madagascar)
* zest of 1 meyer lemon
* 500 g sugar
* 1 L vodka

### Jamaican Nocino

* 2 lb walnuts (about 50-60 walnuts)
* 2 cinnamon sticks
* 8 allspice berries
* 3 star anise
* 1 vanilla bean (madagascar)
* 500 g sugar
* 1 L vodka
